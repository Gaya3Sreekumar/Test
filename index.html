Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM) is inviting applications from IEDCs to form Cluster IEDCs in various Technology Sectors Cluster IEDCs will be theme based IEDCs focusing on one specific sector. The cluster will have IEDCs having interest in the selected domain and lead by one IEDC having expertise and resources in the domain. The objective of the Cluster IEDC is to develop talent in the specific sector in general and start-ups in particular through encouragement of collaborative innovation among students from different institutions. Cluster IEDC encourage IEDCs to open resources to the member IEDCs and enhance the use of resources optimally.
